About Our App

Functional f.IT.ness is an application designed for use by individuals who desire to engage in a challenging and fulfilling workout, but may or may not have access to a gym. Users select the equipment they have access to (multiple pieces of equipment all the way down to no equipment/bodyweight), as well as the desired muscle group they wish to target, and we create a workout plan for that day according to the given input. We understand the importance of breaking out of the monotonous regimen you may currently have and the benefits "switching things up" has on your overall fitness and well-being. We're here to challenge you along your road to greatness and assist you with becomming a healthier, stronger you. We're dedicated to you as long as you are dedicated to yourself! Have a great workout!!!
